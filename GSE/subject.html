Programmes and Services

It is estimated that more and more gifted and talented children are being born each year, and therefore, it will be necessary to implement the relevant tools, in order to allow them to compete academically with their peer groups in developed countries. Therefore, with this view in mind, the management of the Global School of Excellence for Gifted Students, will introduce the educational programmes necessary for assisting them to achieve their highest academic potential. This will be instituted through the following activities:

  • Smaller Class Sizes (low teacher to student ratio)
  • Introduction of multi-grade classrooms
  • Acknowledgement of different learning styles
  • Offering a wide curriculum of study
  • Encouraging students to take part in research and enrichment programmes
  • Facilitating hands-on practical learning and field experiences
  • Promotion of students according to their ability and not because of their age
  • Individualized care and attention
  • Highly trained and qualified teaching staff
