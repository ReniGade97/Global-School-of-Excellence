Scholarship Programme

“A good head and good heart are always a formidable combination. But when you add to that a literate tongue or pen, then you have something very special.” (by Nelson Mandea")

  • Athletics

    With the introduction of the Athletic Scholarship in the school, the students who medal in the Boys and Girls competition will be rewarded financially for their participation.

  • Academic

    The annual academic awarding of scholarships to final year students who have received the highest score and most subject passed in C.X.C, for financial assistance that will greatly assist them to successfully pursue their studies.

  • Leadership

    In an effort to significantly encourage parents to send their children to this school, the management of the institution will give a Leadership Scholarship to the most outstanding boy and girl.

  • Music

    This Scholarship goes towards the choir that wins the "All Together Sing Competition"
